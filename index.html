SPI
We are SPI a technology based college startup. We specialize in Training and Rating under-grad students for appearing in interviews and provide the companies with a chosen few select individuals who meet their criteria.
our aim
In a country like India thousands of students graduate every year. But even though they have the degree most of them do not meet the criteria for working in a company. Many companies waste a ton of resources trying to select the students with necessary technical skills. This is where SPI comes in.
SPI aims to estabilish a rating system based on ones technical skills to help them get their dream job as well as help companies get their dream employees. We aim to provide a detailed summary of a students techical skills which will be calculated using our Skill Proficiency Index to the companies which will help them choose a suitable employee from the large pool of students appearing for job interviews every year.
BENEFITS
Students

SPI rating would really be helpful for students from not so elite colleges like IITs/BITS/NITs to offer their deserving students the same opportunity as them.
It would be of immense help to students who do not have any official degree from a college or are self schooled as due to the IIT culture in India many employers tend to overlook them.

Companies

Companies won't have to utilize their time and resources on large scale interviews and can just focus on the best candidates suitable for their company.
